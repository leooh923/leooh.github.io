Welcome! I am a graduate student in the Department of Statistics at Texas A&M University.

Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of statistical theory, machine learning, and computational methods. I work on developing novel approaches to address challenging problems in statistical inference and prediction.

Statistical Learning

Developing methods for high-dimensional data analysis and variable selection.

Computational Statistics

Creating efficient algorithms for complex statistical models and large-scale inference.

Bayesian Methods

Investigating Bayesian approaches to modern statistical challenges.

Applications

Applying statistical methods to problems in various scientific domains.
